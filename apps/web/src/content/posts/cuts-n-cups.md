Cuts n Cups is a recurring event series held at [FBR Cafe](fbr-cafe). Each session brings together a live music performance with a guided coffee tasting — two things that reward close attention.

The format is simple: a short set, a few coffees prepared different ways, conversation. Sessions are recorded. Bookings open ahead of each event.

The idea is that music and coffee share something — both are better when you slow down and actually listen.
