## Tiramisu Cream

**Ingredients**  
- 975g Mascarpone (Paesanella)  
- 135g White sugar  
- 4 Egg yolks  
- 4 Egg whites  
- 2ml Vanilla extract  
- 1g Shaved Grana Padano  
- 3g Table salt  

**Method**  
1. **Separate & Mix Yolks**  
   - Separate 4 egg yolks and whites into two bowls.  
   - Beat yolks with 7g sugar and 2ml vanilla until thick and ribbon-like.  

2. **Mix Mascarpone**  
   - Add 975g mascarpone and 1g grated Grana Padano to yolks. Mix until smooth.  

3. **Beat Egg Whites**  
   - Clean the mixer, then beat egg whites with remaining 65g sugar until medium peaks form.  

4. **Combine Gently**  
   - Fold whipped egg whites and table salt into yolk-mascarpone mix using a spatula.  
   - Avoid stirring too hard.  

**Done!**

## Coffee Bath

**Ingredients**  
- 33ml Espresso
- 3ml Disaronno  

**Method**  
1. **Prepare Espresso Mix**  
   - Brew 33ml (approx. 1.5 cups) of espresso.  
   - Mix with 3–4 tbsp Disaronno (or preferred liqueur).  

2. **Dip the Biscuits**  
   - Quickly dip 36 Savoiardi biscuits into the mix—max 2 seconds per side to avoid sogginess.  

3. **Assemble Tiramisu**  
   - Layer the dipped biscuits with the tiramisu cream in a container.

## Coffee Jelly

**Ingredients**  
- 5ml Batch brew coffee  
- 3g Agar powder  

**Method**  
1. **Mix & Heat**  
   - Combine 5ml coffee and 3g agar powder in a pot.  
   - Cook over medium-low heat, stirring until agar dissolves (2–3 mins).  

2. **Portion**  
   - While hot, pour 5g into each petri dish or mold.  

3. **Set**  
   - Let cool at room temp, then refrigerate until firm (approx. 1 hour).
