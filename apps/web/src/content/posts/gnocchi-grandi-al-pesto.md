## Ingredients
- cured egg yolk
- garlic oil
- gnocchi
- grated grana padano
- pesto
## Method
1. Boil gnocchi in salted water until gnocchi starts floating
2. While gnocchi is cooking, prepare a petri dish with pesto spread along the bottom
3. Grate grana padano over the pesto and lay 4 gnocchi balls in a diamond shape over the centre of the dish
4. Cut the cured egg yolk in half and lay the two slices on the gnocchi opposite each other facing the same way
5. Add a small drop of garlic oil on each gnocchi and serve
