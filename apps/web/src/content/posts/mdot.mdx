mDOT is a compact MIDI controller built around a single philosophy: one hand, one thought.

Designed for producers, DJs, and sound designers, it strips away the noise of traditional hardware and gives you the controls you actually reach for — mapped exactly how you work.

## What you get

A preorder secures your unit at the early-access price. After purchase, a **product key** will be sent to your email. This key will be used to register your device and access the companion software when units ship.

- Compact form factor — fits in a laptop bag side pocket
- Assignable pads, knobs, and faders
- USB-C bus power, class-compliant (no drivers)
- Deep integration with Ableton, Bitwig, and standalone mode
- Companion app for custom mappings and firmware updates

## Preorder

Units are limited. Keys are issued in order of purchase.
