# Screen Responsiveness

The FBR app will be used across desktop, tablet, and phone screens so it is important that our interface responds to changes in screen sizes.

We want to ensure that no medium is superior to another; user experience should be of the same quality no matter what device they use. 

FBR proposes a rotation mechanism to 'scale' or rather orient the UI according to the screen size. Interface on a desktop and horizontal tablet will look the same. On the phone, the interface will rotate to become portrait.
