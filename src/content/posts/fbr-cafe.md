FBR Cafe is a specialty coffee shop based in Sydney's CBD. We source and serve seasonal single-origin coffees alongside a rotating food menu of simple, well-made things.

The cafe is a gathering space, and the birthplace of [Cuts n Cups](cuts-n-cups), and where most of what FBR does begins. 

We care about ingredients, process, and the people in the room. The coffee changes when something better comes along, and we optimize renlentlessly. The food follows the same pattern. The rest is up to the conversations made between the coffee, the staff, and the patrons.

Running the cafe opened up our eyes to pain points of running a cafe, exactly where inefficeneies and waste comes from. Those insights was what inaitially drove us to the developement of the [Mdot](mdot).
