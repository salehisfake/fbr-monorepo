# FBR ROM — Launch Plan for *The Dial*

## Strategic Positioning

- **Core Narrative**  
  Establish FBR ROM as a technology company operating across two mediums:  
  1. Consumer Hardware  
  2. Sustainable Systems Engineering  
  > Mission: *Reducing waste and doing good things better*

- **Market Positioning**  
  - Do **not** appeal to B2B directly.  
  - Appeal to **baristas and consumers (B2C)**.  
  - Let them organically introduce the product to their businesses.  
  > Create an action, let people find a reason.

- **Investor Narrative**  
  This is a **proof of market** for future investment rounds.  
  Demonstrate demand, price validation, and niche authority.
