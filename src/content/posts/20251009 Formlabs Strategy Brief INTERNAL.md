## Value Propositions  

### For FBR ROM  

- **Integrated Production** – Consistent access to Formlabs hardware shortens design-to-prototype cycles and improves iteration speed.  
- **Narrative Reinforcement** – Embedding additive manufacturing within system storytelling strengthens brand clarity.  
- **Material Research** – Opens experimentation with flexible, clear, and high-precision resins.  
- **Public Credibility** – Association with a recognized industrial brand adds external validation without altering tone.  
- **Media Depth** – Enables cinematic-level documentation of the making process of the M Dot, G string, The Dial and Machine hall

### For Formlabs  

- **System Context:**  
  FBR ROM operates as a functioning ecosystem — engineering, media, and cultural design housed in one process. This differs from Formlabs’ typical user features, which focus on single-discipline creators.  

- **Cultural Integration:**  
  FBR ROM embeds machines into artistic, performative, and public systems. This situates Formlabs hardware within broader cultural narratives rather than isolated use-cases.  

- **High-Design Visual Language:**  
  FBR ROM’s design output and media tone contrast with Formlabs’ current documentary approach.  
  Formlabs’ official media is clean, corporate, and product-driven. FBR ROM offers a cinematic, sensory, and systems-oriented depiction — less "how it works" and more "why it matters."  + its sexy 

- **New Audience Reach:**  
  Partnership extends Formlabs into sectors they rarely address: fashion, coffee and ==@ sebastian 

- **Feedback Value:**  
  FBR ROM’s cross-disciplinary use cases provide detailed feedback on resin behavior, material fatigue, and aesthetic performance under real creative pressure.
