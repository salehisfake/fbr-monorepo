## Ingredients
- fennel  
- focaccia  
- garlic oil
- grana padano  
- pepper  
- prosciutto  
- rocket  
- salt  
- Stracciatella  
- tapenade

## Method
1. Toast focaccia at 18°c for 3 minutes each side; Take out when the crust is crunchy and with inside steaming when cut open
2. Layout on bench crust side down
3. Add two teaspoon dollops of Stracciatella to one slice and 1.5 dollops of tapenade to the other slice
4. Season both sides with pepper and sprinkle on grated grana padano
5. Slice 6g of thin prosciutto and lay on top of the tapenade
6. Combine rocket, fennel shavings, garlic oil, salt and pepper into a container and mix
7. Add salad mix onto the Stracciatella and then combine both sides of sandwich
8. Present and serve
